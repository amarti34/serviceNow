Simple. Functional. Beautiful.

Crafted by some of the world's finest bookmakers, our notebooks are thread bound and lie completely flat. Pens glide across our pages because there is no grain direction, and ink never bleeds through. The result is a truly pleasurable writing experience.

No Trees. No Guilt.

The carbon footprint of stone paper is 60% less than traditional wood-pulp paper and our factories are powered in part by solar energy. Our products are born from a single intention, to respect people and planet. We strive to follow this principle from beginning to end.
